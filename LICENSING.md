# Licensing

The repository uses a multi-license layout:

- Root project code, documentation, configuration, and ForgePilot-specific additions are licensed under the MIT License in `LICENSE`.
- Enterprise-only upstream material under `enterprise/` keeps its original license in `enterprise/LICENSE`.
- Additional community terms for project-specific documents, templates, and brand assets are described in `LICENSE-OPENAGENT-COMMUNITY.md`.

This split keeps the root license easy for GitHub to detect while preserving the license boundaries of upstream and enterprise-only material.
